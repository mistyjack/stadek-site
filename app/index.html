Our Sevices

Aluminium Product

We take pride in the design of aluminium products such as Gliding and Sliding Doors and other aluminium products. We also ensure the product is customized to your taste.

Arc Bending

Our company offers high quality bending services for aluminium profiles with the use of our sophisticated machines. We make every type of arc including Arc Doors and windows and as required.

Installation of Felt Rubber

We install top quality felt rubber that is durable, resilient and unaffected by atmospheric condition and with great grips.

Stainless Steel

We also use stainless steel for our products to give more strength, durability and add to the aesthetic value of the building.

Glass Merchant

We are proficient in the identification of different types of glass such as the tinted glass, plain glass(10mm and 12mm), reflective glass, blue glass, green glass, toughened/tempered glass, frameless door glass which we sell according to the taste of our customers at market price.

Security Fence Wiring

We install razor wire to make you feel secured in your home, stores and other locations as requested. We ensure safe installation to prevent environmental hazard.

Office Partitioning

We specialise in the design and manufacture of demountable office partitions for commercial offices and refurbishment which provide a workspace for each individual with sound proof material for thier privacy.
